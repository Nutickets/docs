The mobile application suite extends the platform's capabilities from the back office to the event floor. These tools are designed for operational staff to manage entry, processing, and sales in physical venue environments.

## The Application Suite

The ecosystem consists of three specialized applications, each serving a distinct operational role:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Access Control Pro" icon="qrcode" href="/user-manual/apps/access-control-pro">
    High-speed ticket validation and entry management. Supports multi-point scanning and real-time attendance tracking.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Box Office Pro" icon="ticket" href="/user-manual/apps/box-office-pro">
    On-the-door ticket sales and reservation management. Allows staff to sell inventory and issue tickets instantly via connected hardware.
  </Card>
  <Card title="EPOS Pro" icon="cash-register" href="/user-manual/apps/epos-pro">
    Point-of-sale system for retail, food, and beverage. Handles product stock, complex orders, and card terminal integrations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
