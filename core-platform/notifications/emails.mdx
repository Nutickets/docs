## Overview

Email notifications are sent both automatically by checkout workflows and manually through admin actions.

## Bulk/Manual Email Actions

The mailer workflow supports audience targeting by events, sale items, schedules, timeslots, and price bands.

Supported bulk email contexts include:

- order confirmations (purchasers or attendees)
- transfer emails
- invoice reminder emails
- payment reminder emails (including payment-link context)
- deposit received reminders

## Template Controls

When sending custom emails you can control:

- title
- subject
- body
- include information vs blank template style

## Queue Behavior

Bulk sends are queued and return an informational message that jobs have been enqueued.
