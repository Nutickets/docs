## Overview

Resale fees are implemented as fee sale items and can be configured globally (company source) or per event.

## Scope Options

- **Company resale fees**: reusable defaults managed from resale fee listing.
- **Event resale fees**: event-specific resale fee behavior.

## Configuration Inputs

- currencies and pricing
- tax band / conditional tax settings
- applicable channels
- restricting item types where relevant

## Permissions and Feature Gates

Managing resale fees requires resale access and fee permissions. Some integration modes may restrict order-level fee behavior.
