## Overview

- Articles are managed content entities with draft/published status.
- Articles belong to article types and can be tagged.
- Card/page media and SEO metadata are supported.

## Management Workflow

1. Create article types.
2. Create article records with author/type/tags.
3. Edit content blocks and page settings.
4. Publish and surface through article blocks/listings.
