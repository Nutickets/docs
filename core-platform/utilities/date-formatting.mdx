## Overview

Date and time formatting is centrally configurable through company settings.

## Date Display Options

- ISO
- Middle-endian
- Big-endian
- Little-endian

## Time Display Options

- ISO
- 12-hour
- 24-hour

## Timezone Display

A separate setting controls whether timezone information is shown to users.

## Best Practice

Pick one display standard per company and keep it consistent across training, reporting, and customer comms.
