## Overview

- Instant checkout surfaces payment actions directly from the basket context.
- Staff can optionally include or exclude current basket behaviour before triggering payment.
- This is designed for high-throughput assisted selling.

## Panel Behaviour

| Control | Behaviour |
|---|---|
| Customer search | Assign/change basket customer in-line |
| Include in basket toggle | Controls basket handling for instant action |
| Action list | Shows eligible payment method shortcuts |

## Operational Notes

- Availability depends on box-office mode and method configuration.
- Some company limitations can disable instant-checkout capability entirely.
